About Us

Welcome to My Nihongo Path, your companion in the journey of learning the Japanese language.

Our Mission

We aim to make learning Japanese Hiragana and vocabulary accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone. Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to brush up on your skills, our interactive tools are designed to help you succeed.

Fan-Made Project

"This website is a personal project created by a Japanese language enthusiast. It is an independent, fan-made platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any government agency or official educational institution."

This project was born out of a passion for the Japanese language and culture. It serves as a tool for self-study and community learning.
